سفر الظلال في بحر النهار (Shadows travelling on the sea of the day), 2022, is a site-specific artwork located at the desert outside of Al Zubarah and Ain Mohammed heritage sites in Qatar's northernmost region. This film documents the conceptual and installation process of the artwork.
