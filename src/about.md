## PROFILE

I am a tech savvy creative director, filmmaker and production lead with over 20 years' experience shaping high-profile content from long-form documentary to short-form online-first content for global brands, agencies, and international organisations. I have worked on projects for clients including M&C Saatchi, Accenture, McKinsey, The World Bank, Google, UNHCR, BBC, Arte, Channel4, CNN and Al-Jazeera.

I am fluent in English, French and German. I also have a basic knowledge of Arabic and its culture, having worked across the Arab World on several assignements. I have an excellent knowledge of digital and film (35mm & 16mm) workflows, formats and know how to shoot, light and edit.
